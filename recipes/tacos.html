Tacos

Description

Delicious ground beef with mexican spices.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. Dice onion and pepper
  2. Brown beef, drain fat and remove from heat
  3. Soften onion and pepper in same skillet
  4. Add beef and taco seasoning to skillet
  5. Add 1/4C water and simmer until liquid evaporates
