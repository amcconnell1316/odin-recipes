Lasagna

Description

Cheesy soft layered pasta.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Pasta sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In 9x13 baking pan, layer pasta noodles, ricotta cheese and pasta sauce
  2. Cover with mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  3. Bake at 350F for 60 mins
  4. Remove when liquid is bubbling and cheese is brown
